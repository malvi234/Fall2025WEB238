Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official site?
No — this is a student project inspired by the Frans Hals Museum aesthetic and built to demonstrate jQuery enhancements.
How were the interactions made?
jQuery selectors (including filters like :odd and :has()) plus effects like slideToggle() and fadeIn() power the UI.
Can I filter by Hogwarts house?
Yes — use the chips above the gallery to show only Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, or Hufflepuff items.
